About the Platform

The Alumni Networking Platform connects students and alumni to support mentorship, career development, and community growth. It offers tools for guided learning, job readiness, and shared success.

Key Features

Mentorship with Alumni

Students get connected with experienced alumni for career guidance and personal development.

Learn and Earn: Internship

Start your journey with internships to gain real-world experience and practical skills.

Learn and Earn: Job

After internship experience, students can apply for relevant job opportunities through the platform.

Learn and Earn: Freelancing

Freelancing becomes accessible once users have job experience, ensuring readiness and professionalism.

Fundraising

Alumni can contribute financially to support scholarships, events, and platform development initiatives.

Feedback

Alumni provide feedback to help universities evaluate and improve academic and career services.
